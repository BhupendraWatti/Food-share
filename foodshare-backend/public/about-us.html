Our Story

FoodShare was founded by a group of passionate individuals who were concerned about the growing food waste and hunger in our communities. What started as a small initiative to collect surplus food from local restaurants has now grown into a comprehensive food rescue and redistribution program.

Our journey began when our founder, Bhupendra Watti, witnessed large amounts of perfectly good food being thrown away at a local restaurant. Determined to make a difference, she approached the restaurant owner with a simple proposal: instead of throwing away the food, donate it to those in need.

Today, FoodShare works with hundreds of food donors and volunteers to rescue and redistribute food to shelters, community centers, and individuals in need.

Our Mission

At FoodShare, we believe that no one should go hungry while perfectly good food goes to waste. Our mission is to create a sustainable food system where surplus food is redistributed to those who need it most.

Reduce Food Waste

We work to reduce food waste by collecting surplus food from restaurants, grocery stores, and events.

Feed the Hungry

We distribute rescued food to shelters, community centers, and individuals in need.

Raise Awareness

We educate the community about food waste and hunger through workshops and events.
