About FoodShare

We are dedicated to reducing food waste and hunger in our communities through food rescue and redistribution.

Our Mission

At FoodShare, we believe that no one should go hungry while perfectly good food goes to waste. Our mission is to create a sustainable food system where surplus food is redistributed to those who need it most.

Join us in our journey to create a world where everyone has access to nutritious food and nothing goes to waste.
